UpsightLoggerLevel

Every log message has an associated severity level. The log reporter uses the level to determine which messages should be displayed in the output window. You set the reporter’s level with the defaultLogLevel: method of . Any message with a level equal to or more severe than the setting is displayed. The log levels described below are ordered from most to least severe.
